Cipherguard v4.10.2 is part of a long series of maintenance updates aimed at laying the groundwork for the upcoming v5 release and addressing bugs reported by the community. This update ensures the browser extension is fully compatible with the new v5 resource formats, allowing developers to prepare their integrations under the best possible conditions.

Thank you to the community for your reports.
